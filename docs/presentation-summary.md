## Presentation Tips

1. **Start Strong**: Open with the problem statement and market size
2. **Show, Don't Tell**: Use live demo or video walkthrough
3. **Emphasize AWS**: Highlight Bedrock integration and AWS services
4. **Data-Driven**: Back claims with metrics and benchmarks
5. **Visual Appeal**: Use high-quality screenshots and diagrams
6. **Tell a Story**: Follow a retail manager's daily workflow
7. **End with Impact**: Social and economic benefits
8. **Call to Action**: Invite to try demo, join beta, or partner
